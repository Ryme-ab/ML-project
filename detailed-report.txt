Notebooks (key files)
---------------------
- notebooks/preprocessing/01_data_loading_and_cleaning.ipynb: Raw data ingestion and cleaning (Comtrade, World Bank, WTO). Executable checkpoints exist.
- notebooks/preprocessing/02_feature_engineering.ipynb: Feature engineering for country and sector-level features.
- notebooks/preprocessing/03_scaling_and_normalization.ipynb: Scaling, standardization and robust transforms.
- notebooks/preprocessing/04_master_integration_and_eda.ipynb: Master table integration and exploratory data analysis.
- notebooks/preporocessing/*_checkpoint.ipynb: Checkpoint notebooks for incremental data loading (typo'd folder name preserved).
- notebooks/classification/01_product_opportunity_classification.ipynb: Classification workflow to label opportunities.
- notebooks/classification/02_market_opportunity_classification.ipynb: Market-level classification analysis.
- notebooks/clustring/clustering_model.ipynb: Clustering experiments and cluster assignment outputs.
- notebooks/clustring/* output and reports may accompany clustering notebook in `reports/` and `notebooks/.../output/`.
